About The Governance Gazette

Policy. Power. Public Life.

The Governance Gazette is an independent publication covering governance, policy, economy, and public affairs in India. We are committed to rigorous, original analysis that goes beyond headlines to explain what events mean for India's democracy, economy, and society.

At the heart of The Governance Gazette is The Collector's Corner — our dedicated Civil Services Desk for UPSC aspirants. Every major policy development we cover is analysed through the lens of the Civil Services examination, with GS paper tagging, PYQ linkages, answer frameworks, and model answers.

We believe that the best way to understand India's governance is to understand it deeply — not just what happened, but why it happened, what constitutional or legal framework governs it, and what it means for ordinary citizens.
