About The Governance Gazette

Policy. Power. Public Life. — India's independent governance and civil services publication, founded April 2026.

The Publication

The Governance Gazette is an independent digital publication covering governance, policy, economy, and public affairs in India. Launched in April 2026, it is built on the conviction that rigorous, structured analysis — not just news aggregation — is what serious readers and aspirants need.

At the heart of the Gazette is The Collector's Corner — a dedicated Civil Services Desk for UPSC aspirants. Every major policy development is mapped to the Civil Services examination syllabus, with GS paper tagging, PYQ linkages, answer frameworks, and model answers written for aspirants who want depth, not just headlines.

The Gazette operates on a free-first model. All governance, economy, foreign policy, environment, and editorial content is freely accessible to everyone. Premium subscribers unlock the full Civil Services Desk experience — weekly current affairs PDFs, GS-tagged deep dives, answer writing models, and editorial breakdowns delivered every week.
